About Us
OUR VISION

To become an internationally acclaimed technology based institute that improves access to education while maintaining the highest quality standards.

OUR MISSION
  • To provide the highest quality of education to all aspiring students irrespective of their age, gender, religion, and geographical location.
  • To train students to grow in their professional lives and inculcate an entrepreneurial mindset with high ethical and moral standards to become a productive part of society.
  • To provide the best working environment to faculty and staff to create a culture of research, innovation and cooperation.
  • To promote the philosophy of knowledge sharing by providing free and unhindered access to all of its educational content.

Rules and Regulations

The purpose of developing these rules and regulations is to make the parents and students aware of the institution's policy. The student will abide by these rules and regulations.

1. Punctuality is mandatory.

2. All students must arrive at the institution at least 5 minutes before the start of the class.

3. Must participate in homework and all tests.

4. Bring all necessary stationery and text workbook.

5. Mobile phones will not be allowed in the institution. Mobile phones will be confiscated in case of violation.

6. Payment of monthly fee will be in advance.

7. Fee once paid is non-refundable.

8. In case of breach of discipline and any misdemeanor, admission will be canceled.

9. The leave application signed by the parents has to be submitted to the institution.

10. Compensation must be paid in case of damage to the property of the institution.
